ABOUT SWITZERLAND

A

Land of The Majestic Alps

It is believed that the name of this distinctive country comes from the Old High German word Swēizzar, originally referring to its people.

Switzerland is famous for its majestic Alps, crystal-clear lakes, picturesque villages, and lush green meadows. Its breathtaking beauty is simply beyond words!

Switzerland is also known for its stunning landscapes, skiing, hiking, rich history, and diverse culture. These attractions draw travelers from around the world year-round.
